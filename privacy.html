1. Who We Are

Stoken Games LLC is a game design and software development company based in Georgia, United States. We design strategy board games and mobile applications. You can contact us at info@stoken.games.

2. Information We Collect

Website stoken.games

Waitlist Signup

If you submit your email address via the Kinterpret waitlist form, we collect your email address to notify you when the app launches. We use Formspree to process these submissions. We will not send you marketing emails beyond app launch notifications without your explicit consent.

Contact Form

When you submit the contact form, we collect your name, email address, optional company name, and message content in order to respond to your inquiry.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not use advertising tracking or sell your data to third parties
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not create user accounts on this website

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide and improve our services:

  • To send you a single notification when Kinterpret launches (waitlist only)
  • To respond to your contact form inquiries

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

Formspree

We use Formspree to process waitlist and contact form submissions. Your email address and message content are processed by Formspree in accordance with their privacy policy.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid legal process.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@stoken.games.
