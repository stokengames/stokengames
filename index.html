Our Game Library

Each game is designed to teach real strategic concepts through engaging, competitive gameplay. Used in corporate training, classrooms, and living rooms worldwide.

Supply Chain Strategy

Greenfield

Build your supply chain and brand to become the market leader in supply chain agility. Greenfield simulates strategic supply chain decisions that lead companies to real success — and punishes timid play and over-zealous strategies. Featured in Nike's North American Supply Chain training program.

Supply Chain Strategy

Emergent

Enter the business world as the president of a new company competing for consumers' money. Build your distribution center network across the US map and outmaneuver competitors in this deep supply chain network strategy game with game theory elements.

As a mentor for 5 young budding professionals, can you develop your people and get them to the top of the organization? Give them the experiences they need to grow while navigating the real challenges of corporate career development.
