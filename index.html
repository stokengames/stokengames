Custom Builds

Bespoke games where every teammate is the main character. Designed to print, built to keep.

Analytics Card Battle is a custom trading card game built for a 20-person enterprise analytics team. Every teammate gets their own card — custom chibi art, real stats sourced from a short survey, two signature "powers," and a fun fact.

During the live event, scenarios project on screen and players pair up to combine cards. Bonus points trigger automatically when a scenario keyword matches one of a player's powers, so the storyteller and the supply-chain expert pull different value from the same crisis.

Cards print on professional trading-card stock at the standard 2.5×3.5 inch size — the kind that keeps showing up in someone's desk drawer two years later. The format flexes: built here for analytics, just as easily a sales kickoff, an onboarding ritual, or an off-site that needs a centerpiece.
